Who are we?

SyncSonic is an innovative group of four experienced UK-based songwriters, instrumentalists and producers, each bringing our individual skills to all projects we venture into. Our mission is to create top-tier bespoke compositions tailored to the diverse needs of our clientele across a range of genres, and platforms. We specialise in commissions for original music in television, film, advertisements and other visual media, which we do with a fresh and unique approach. At SyncSonic, we pride ourselves on our ability to maintain absolute trust with our clients, elevate their narratives, and leave a positive mark on brand identities.
